Pan Seared Chicken Cutlets

Description

Second on my list of easy meals for the working adult, pan seared chicken is not only quick, but tasty!

Ingredients

  • One Chicken Cutlet
  • Seasoning Salt
  • Olive Oil

Steps

  1. Turn Stove on High, place skillet on stove.
  2. Add Olive Oil to skillet.
  3. Lay Chicken Cutlets on pan, careful to avoid hot oil.
  4. Sear each side, flipping as needed, adding seasoning salt to both sides in pinches.
  5. Keep searing both sides of chicken until golden-brown crust starts to form.
  6. Once golden-brown crust forms, turn off stove and use tongs to place chicken onto plate. Let cool and enjoy.
